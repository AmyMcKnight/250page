Hi there! Thanks for stopping by to take a peak at my page. This page was actually the final project for my Skillcrush 101: HTML & CSS class I was in the September 2015 Web Developer Blueprint.

That all keeps me pretty busy. But I still find time to hangout with hubby and our little girl, and to make artisan soap.
